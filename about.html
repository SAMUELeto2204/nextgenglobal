About Our Organization

NextGen Global Initiatives Inc. is a community-focused organization based in Saskatoon dedicated to empowering families and supporting child development through education, childcare programs, and community initiatives.

Our organization works to strengthen communities by creating programs that support children, families, and future leaders.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To empower families and communities by providing programs that support early learning, childcare, and community development.

Vision: To build a strong and inclusive community where children and families have access to opportunities that help them grow and succeed.

Leadership

Mary Abiola Adeagbo

Executive Director

Mary Abiola Adeagbo is a passionate early childhood educator, nutritionist, and community advocate. She leads NextGen Global Initiatives with a vision to empower families, support children’s development, and strengthen communities through education, care, and social programs. She holds an Early Childhood Education (ECE) certification from SaskPolytechnic.

Oluwaseun Adeagbo

Operations Manager

Oluwaseun Adeagbo oversees operations and strategic development at NextGen Global Initiatives. With experience in project coordination, community engagement, and organizational management, he supports the mission of building sustainable programs that positively impact families, youth, and communities. He also holds an Early Childhood Education (ECE) certification from SaskPolytechnic.
